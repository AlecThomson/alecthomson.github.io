Research

My research is focused on investigations of magnetism in galaxies. Currently, I am working with the latest polarization surveys in order to better understand the interactions between Galactic magnetic fields and structures within the Milky May.

I completed my PhD at the Australian National University, supervised by Prof. Naomi McClure-Griffiths, A/Prof. Christoph Federrath, and Prof. John Dickey.
